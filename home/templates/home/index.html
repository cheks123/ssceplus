Science and Mathematics

Mathematics

Mathematics is a core subject in the school curriculum. It is required that every student gets at least a credit pass in the subject in order to be eligible for admission into the higher institutions. In this section, you are provided with materials that will enable you excel in subject.

Physics

Physics is essential for anyone who wants to venture into the field of engineering, astronomy and science. It enables us to unravel matter and its relation to energy. Join us to explore gravity, electricity, magnetism, light, sound and lots more.

Start Learning

Chemistry

Chemistry is a science subject that deals with the property, constituent and uses of matter. It is an essential subject for anyone that want to go into the field of engineering, medicine and science. Join us to explore length and breadth of matter.

Biology

Biology is the study of living things, plants and animals, and their environment. It is an interesting thing to study the innumerable number of living things ranging from micro-organisms like viruses and bacteria to macro-organisms like the blue whales, elephants and human.

Further Mathematics

This subject is an extension of General Mathematics. Here, we cover more advanced topics like calculus, permutation and combination, probability distributions etc. It is advisable for learners who want to study engineering, science or social science courses in the university to take the subject.
